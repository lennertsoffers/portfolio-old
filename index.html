About me

A motivated student

I'm a hardworking developer with a strong interest in new technologies and enjoy working on group projects. During my time as a student at Thomas More, I've been busy with lots of things:

  • Participant in several hackathons and won a few, including RealDolmen's "de Warmest Hackathon" and "Hack The Future" from Cronos.
  • President of the student council of IT Factory, member of the General Student Council (ASR), delegate of Thomas More Kempen in the Flemish Association of Students (VVS).
  • Participant in the ICE Honours program of ICE Cube (ICE = Innovation, Creativity & Entrepreneurship). Where I was the 'President of Enactus Thomas More Kempen', which meant that I kept oversight and reported to Enactus Belgium.
